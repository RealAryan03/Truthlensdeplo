Responsible AI Principles

TruthLens is built to support critical thinking, not replace human judgment. The platform surfaces evidence signals, confidence context, and explainable outputs to help users verify claims responsibly.

We continuously improve model quality, calibrate scoring thresholds, and prioritize transparency in how verdicts are generated.
