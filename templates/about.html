About TruthLens

Credibility Intelligence for the Modern News Cycle

TruthLens helps readers assess article reliability using machine learning signals and external fact-check context. The goal is simple: make verification fast, transparent, and understandable before misinformation spreads.

Our Mission

To strengthen digital trust by providing timely credibility insights that support safer information decisions.

Design Philosophy

Human-centered and evidence-first: every score is paired with explainable context, not opaque verdicts.

Audience

Built for journalists, students, researchers, and public users navigating fast-moving information ecosystems.

Machine Learning Analysis

Evaluates writing patterns and linguistic cues that commonly separate reliable reporting from misleading narratives.

Fact-Check Integration

Cross-references key extracted claims with verified public fact-check databases to add external validation.

Actionable Scoring

Converts model and fact-check evidence into a clear credibility score so users can decide what to trust next.

Methodology Snapshot

The platform performs linguistic signal extraction, claim-level evidence checks, and confidence-weighted scoring. Results are intended for guidance and should be interpreted alongside source evaluation and contextual review.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

TruthLens is committed to responsible data handling. This policy describes what information is processed, how it is used, and the safeguards applied in normal operation.

  • Data We Process: submitted article text for credibility analysis and optional contact details for support communication.
  • Purpose Limitation: data is used strictly for analysis output generation, product quality checks, and user support response.
  • Security Measures: standard technical and operational safeguards are used to protect processed information.
  • No Legal Certification: platform scores are informational and do not represent legal verification or legal advice.

Terms of Use

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

By using TruthLens, users acknowledge that outputs are advisory signals designed to assist evaluation workflows. Final editorial, academic, and professional decisions remain the user's responsibility.

  • Permitted Use: personal, educational, and organizational credibility review use-cases.
  • Decision Responsibility: users must independently verify high-impact claims before publication or action.
  • Service Scope: model and fact-check outputs may evolve as datasets, APIs, and scoring methods are improved.
  • Limitation: TruthLens is provided as an analytical aid and does not guarantee absolute factual correctness.
